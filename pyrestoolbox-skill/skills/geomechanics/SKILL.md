# Geomechanics Skill

Focused on wellbore stability, stress analysis, and fracture mechanics.

## When to Use
Invoke this skill for:
- **Pore Pressure**: Eaton's method for prediction from well logs.
- **Stress Analysis**: Vertical (overburden), horizontal, and effective stresses.
- **Mud Weight Window**: Safe window for drilling, breakout, and collapse risks.
- **Fracture Mechanics**: Fracture gradient, breakdown pressure, width estimation.
- **Rock Strength**: UCS from logs, elastic moduli, stress polygon analysis.

## Workflow
1. Identify the depth of interest and known gradients (hydrostatic, overburden).
2. Calculate the Pore Pressure using Eaton's correlation.
3. Estimate horizontal stresses (minimum/maximum).
4. Determine the mud weight limits to avoid borehole failure or hydraulic fracturing.
5. Provide a summary of safe operating limits for drilling.
